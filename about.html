WHO WE ARE

Pacific Health was founded by Ross Rieger in 1984 to assist people to live happier, healthier lives.

At Pacific Health we are set up to supply and support natural health professionals and their customers. We offer a wide variety of products including vitamin supplements, herbal remedies, homeopathic preparations, essential oils, flower essences and massage oils and aim to enable our professional customers with a wide variety of options to cover all contingencies. We source quality products from respected suppliers mainly throughout New Zealand and Australia but also out of the US and the UK.

Pacific Health supports our professional customers with an expert technical team who are available to offer advice and information on the products we provide, as well as support with tricky cases and protocols.
